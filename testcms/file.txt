Core: two or three behavioral traits that are inherent in an organization and do not change overtime and must already exist. They should be usedto guide every aspect of an org, from hiring andfiring to strategy and performance management.<br />
<br />
Aspirational: characteristics an org wants to haveand believes it must develop in order to maximizeits success in its current market environment.<br />
<br />
Accidental: traits the are evident in an org but havecome about unintentionally and don’t necessarilyserve the good of the org.<br />
<br />
Permission-to-Play: minimum behavioral standardsthat are required in an organization. Althoughthey are important, they don’t clearly defineor differentiate an org from others. Things likehonesty, integrity, and respect or others fit into this category.
