Whether you're just starting out or have been freelancing for years,

knowing the art of self promotion is important to keep your business moving forward.

Some designers focus on self promotion more than others, but smart freelancers know their audience and where they hang out. It's important to reach out to clients consistently and follow up on warm leads. Sharing useful information, staying visible, publishing your work, and asking for the project should be top priority.

Get the projects you want

All too often freelancers get busy and forget to market themselves leaving themselves high and dry with no work. Eventually freelancers end up scrambling to get work by taking projects they're not comfortable with just to pay the bills. Long term, taking on unwanted projects isn't healthy for your business and may take focus away from your primary business goals.

Create an effective system

Imagine having an established system for self promotion that keeps your pipeline full and your wallet fat. A way to level off the peaks and valleys of income fluctuation. You'll look back over time and wonder why you didn't develop a system sooner. We're all so busy with work and looking ahead to new projects, but there are few things more important to long term success than consistent self promotion.

More leads = more business

This course will help you take your existing work, clients, and network to create an effective self promotion system you can use for years to come. It takes simple concepts you can implement on your own to create more leads which lead to more business and profit.
